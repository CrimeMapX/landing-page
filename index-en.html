Frequently Asked Questions

We answer your questions about CrimeMapX and our security analysis services

Where does your data come from?

We use official data from the Ministry of Interior and other verified government sources. All data is processed and regularly updated to ensure accuracy and relevance.

How often is the data updated?

Basic data is updated quarterly, coinciding with official publications. Premium users have access to more frequent updates and real-time data when available.

How do you calculate the security index?

Our security index is calculated using an algorithm that considers multiple factors: types of crimes, frequency, severity, area population, and historical trends. Each factor has a specific weight in the final calculation.

Can I integrate your data into my real estate website?

Yes, we offer an API and customizable widgets to integrate our data into real estate portals. Contact our sales team to learn about available options and get a demo.

Is the data reliable for making real estate decisions?

Yes, our data is used by real estate professionals throughout Spain. However, we recommend using our information as one tool in the decision-making process, along with other relevant factors.
